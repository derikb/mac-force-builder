
MACS:
- Modules:
  - 1-Main gun (max class + 1)
  - 2-6  (max 3 of same hardware) (other guns <= class in power)
  - max 2 brawl weapons must be same

AuxUnits:
- 2 max weapons
- max 2 dupes of same hardware
- Infantry
  - can have Power 1 weapons
  - formations 1-5 units
- Vehicles
  - can have Power 2 weapons
  - formations 1-3 units


Weapons:
- Range: S, L, A
- Power: 1-4
  - MACs can have 1 weapon at (Class + 1) Power as Module 1, other weapons are limited to (Class) power.
  - AuxUnits
- Type: B, P, G, M
- Subtype: X, T, J, R
  - X can be compbined with other subtypes

Brawl:
- P2, B2, G2, M2
- plus optional subtype

Hardware:
- Types M/I/V/A
  - M: MAC only
  - I: Infantry only
  - V: Vehicle only
  - A: All types
- MACs cannot have more than three of the same module

- faction
  - special faction modules and rules
- name
- cost
- division (a/b/c)
  - Each division must have a MAC
  - No division may have more than half the units
- initiative
- internal damage (1-3)
- module damage (2 checks)
