About / Help

About

A web app (coded by me, Derik Badman) for creating forces for MAC Attack, a tabletop miniature game by Chris McDowall.

Help

  • Data is stored on your current/local browser. As such there is no guarantee of it sticking around from one use to the next.
  • EXPORT YOUR FORCES if you want to assure you can keep them.
